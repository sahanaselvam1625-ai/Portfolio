Online Shopping Website

Overview

The Stylez is a responsive and visually appealing fashion shopping website that provides users with an elegant interface to explore and purchase stylish dresses. The website aims to give users a smooth online shopping experience by highlighting trending collections, featured products, and easy navigation options.

Features

Homepage

Eye-catching banner with a “Shop Now” button and responsive navigation.

Featured Products

Stylish product cards showcasing top items with images and prices.

Trending Collections

Grid layout displaying the latest fashion trends with elegant visuals.

About Section

Engaging brand story with profile image and smooth animations.

Footer Section

Minimal footer with copyright and brand credits.
